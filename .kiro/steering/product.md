# Product Overview

**Agents.MD** is an advanced cryptocurrency trading intelligence platform that provides real-time market analysis, AI-powered trading signals, and regulatory news monitoring. The platform focuses on Bitcoin and Ethereum analysis with a distinctive newspaper-style interface.

## Core Features

- **Real-time Trading Zones**: Live supply/demand analysis using order book data from Binance
- **AI-Powered Price Predictions**: Multi-timeframe analysis with confidence scoring
- **Regulatory Intelligence**: Automated monitoring of crypto regulatory changes
- **Technical Analysis**: RSI, MACD, EMA, Bollinger Bands with live calculations
- **Market Sentiment**: Fear & Greed Index integration and whale movement detection

## Target Users

Professional traders, crypto enthusiasts, and institutions requiring sophisticated market intelligence with a focus on actionable trading insights.

## Design Philosophy

The platform uses a classic newspaper aesthetic to present complex financial data in an accessible, professional format that builds trust and credibility.
